—Our Top Tier

2023 Audi R8

The R8 is a supercar for those who care as much about making an entrance at the end of their journey as the thrills they might have on the journey.

2018 RR Phantom VIII

The Phantom is always filled with touches of luxury. One of the important things that differentiates the seventh generation from the eighth is the use of the latest aluminium material which will be the basis for making Rolls-Royce cars in the future.

2024 Porsche 911 Turbo

The 911 Turbo may look like other versions of Porsche’s iconic sports car, but its ballistic performance puts it in the same stratosphere as supercars from Ferrari and Lamborghini.

2025 Chevrolet Corvette Z06

With its extraordinary mid-engine performance, exotic-car looks and personality, and hard-to-believe value, the Corvette Z06 is a sterling example of what GM can do when the engineers are given a long leash.

2025 Bentley Continental GT

The 2025 GT gets new high-tech dual-valve adaptive dampers, which are said to provide a wider range and should enable both a plusher ride in Comfort mode and a tighter rein on body motions in the most aggressive Sport mode.

2024 Porsche Macan EV

The Macan EV is a rare electric SUV in that it can hug corners like a Porsche sports car. Athletic handling, good driving range, fast charging times on a DC connection.
